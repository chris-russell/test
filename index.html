Keyboard Instructions

Drag variables from the repository to the numerator and denominator to define the equation for the estimated time that reserves will remain.

If mouse drag and drop functionality is unavailable, press tab or shift+tab on your keyboard to select the variables.

Press spacebar or enter or control+M to pick up the variable.

Press tab or shift+tab or arrow keys to move the focus to the target locations.

Press spacebar or enter or control+M to drop the variable or use the escape key to cancel.
